Overall Site

Overall, the building will be equiped with many facilities. It will be a great location for girl guide trips and tourism.

The building is two floors and a basement. The basement is filled with staff accomidationa and sitting room.
There is a seperate building from the dorms which holds all the activities equipment.
The building is also within a 30 minute walking distance of Galway city.

Building Facilities

The facilities that will be provided are:

  • Rooms to hold up to 150 people with rooms for 30 staff
  • Two dining rooms and sitting rooms
  • Pool Table Room
  • Swimming Pool
  • Rock Climing Wall
  • Walking Route
  • Fire Pit
  • Outdoor Cooking Area
  • Carpark
