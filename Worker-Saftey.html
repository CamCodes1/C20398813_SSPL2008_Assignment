Steps Taken for Saftey

There are many steps taken to ensure saftey for employees who work on site. These steps are:

  • Equipment Trainings
  • Having Authorised Person Areas Only
    • By having these steps, it controls unsafe areas on site but also protects employees from harm.

    Mandatory Trainings to Work on Site

    There are many trainings for employees. These trainings are:

  • Equipment Trainings
  • Basic First Aid
  • Safepass Trainings
    • By having these trainings in place, it ensures the site staff are prepared for any situation that occurs on site.

    Emergency Procedures

    All employees must know the emergency procedures on site.
    These procedures include things such as knowing fire procedures.
    By having set procedures to follow for certain situations, it keeps site workers safe.
