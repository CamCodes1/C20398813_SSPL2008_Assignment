Overall Porject Aim

This website display's the construction process and on site saftey for the building of a new centre for the Irish Girl Guides.
As there are 5 world centres for the IGG, another one is going to be made within Ireland.
This world centre will be based on the world centre in Switzerland which is Our Chalet.
The chosen location for this centre will be Galway, which benefits tousim in the west of Ireland.

Current World Centres

Kusafiri

This world centre is based in Africa.
It is the only centre that moves around the continent

Our Cabana

This world centre is based in Mexico.
It has been a world centre since 1957.

Our Chalet

This world centre is based in the Alps in Switzerland.
Our Chalet has been an official world centre since 1932.

Pax Lodge

This world centre is based in London, UK.
It became a world centre in 1991.

Sangam

This world centre is based in Pune, India.
Sangam has been a world centre since 1966.
